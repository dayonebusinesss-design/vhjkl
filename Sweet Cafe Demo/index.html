OUR STORY

A COZY RETREAT

Located on the B2 Floor of the Intercity Hotel in Al Jaddaf, Sweet Art is a cozy sanctuary for coffee lovers and pastry enthusiasts. We blend classic techniques with modern flavors to create an unforgettable culinary experience.

Whether you're stopping by for your morning brew, a hearty breakfast, or our highly praised caramel eclairs, we promise an atmosphere of refined elegance and impeccable quality.
